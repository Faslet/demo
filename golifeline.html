Buxton ladies hardshell jacket

The Buxton is a nice basic hardshell jacket and ideal for all your outdoor activities. Because the jacket is of supple quality, it gives you complete freedom of movement. It protects you against a bump of wind and is also water-repellent and breathable. The jacket also has a hood which can be put on by means of a cord. The jacket can be tied at the waist on the inside, so you can create a nice tailored effect. The jacket has a zip and popper fastening and there are two closable zippers at the front to store your important belongings. Due to the waterproof materials, the Buxton can also be used as a raincoat. 

 

Features :

  • Waterproof material (WC5000)
  • Breathable (MC3000)
  • Water repellent
  • Lightweight
  • Foldable
  • Taped seams

Quality : 100% Polyester
