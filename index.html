The raincoat has cuffs that are specially shaped and tailored to your hands for optimal protection.

The Rain trench has a keycord and spacious inner pockets with a headphone connection eyelet for the headphones.

The functional raincoat has a broad and high collar, storm fastening, taped seams to protect the jacket from rain, a detachable hood and pockets with waterproof zippers.

The back has a pleat for easy movement.

The coat also has special ventilation openings that are placed under the armpits.

The style is cool and because of its timeless appearance it is a raincoat that can be worn for years. These functional features provide the ultimate comfort to wear this coat daily and throughout all seasons.

Characteristics & Smart Features.

- 100% waterproof: a breathable lamination has been applied with a water column of 10.000

- DWR treatment: the durable water-resistant outer treatment, does not let the rainproof fabric soak in moisture.

- Taped seams: all of the seams are taped. 

- 100% windproof: due to the breathable lamination every waterproof coat functions as a windbreaker as well

- breathable (5000 gr./m2): you can wear this raincoat on a daily-basis regardless the weather conditions because the applied lamination is breathable and this will surely make you feel comfortable and confident at any time of the year.

- Air ventilations under the armpits: two at each side for extra air circulation, when you are in a rush.

- Side Pockets with zippers and keycord

- hood with cap- adjustable at 3 points: protect your hairdo after all the morning efforts. We have tested all our hoods throughout many sassy bike rides across Amsterdam, and both attached and detachable versions are very much suitable in the unbearable weather conditions.

- branded bodywarmer buttons: to be able to attach the color-blocking & reversible bodywarmers for the colder seasons 

- inside pocket with flap-button closure and headphone connection eyelet: secure your important belongings & cards in here. The headphone connection eyelet allows you to store your smartphone and listen to music with ease at the same time.

 - 2-way zipper for easy movement and biking.

 

Composition & Sizing. 

- shell: 100% breathable Polyester in colour navy, that has a subtle shiny woven surface. This shell is a lightweight fabric, which means it is suitable for the adventurous summer holidays, during the in-between-seasons.

- lining: 100% polyester with a soft & sleek touch.

- sizing: The coat has a tailored fit.

- designed in Amsterdam & made ethically with Fair Labour in PRC.

 

Cleaning

 - machine washable:(sensitive detergent, short & gentle program of 15 min, do not soak)

- speciality gentle dry cleaning: search for the sign of 'F with double-underscore sign': cleaning with sensitive solvents used for leather goods. Or pass by our well-trusted dry-cleaner, Cleaning Shop Express located at Huidenstraat 24A in Amsterdamfor an overall cleaning.
